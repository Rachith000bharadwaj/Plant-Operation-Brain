# Near-Miss Incident Report — NM-2026-03

Incident ID: NM-2026-03
Date: 2026-01-08
Equipment involved: P-7 (Cooling Water Pump)
Type: Water hammer during mechanical seal replacement

## Description
During seal replacement on P-7, the discharge valve DV-7 was left open because
the work permit template only instructed closing the suction valve SV-7. When the
line was disturbed, water hammer occurred, narrowly missing a technician.

## Root Cause
Outdated lockout procedure in circulation that did not require closing the
discharge valve. Cross-reference: RCA-2025-44 (alignment), maintenance log P-7.

## Corrective Action
Update all P-7 work permit templates to require closing BOTH suction and
discharge valves before seal work. Re-train shift crews.
