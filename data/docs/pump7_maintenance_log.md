# Maintenance Log — Centrifugal Pump P-7 (Cooling Water Circuit)

Equipment Tag: P-7
Location: Cooling Water Pump House, Bay 2
OEM: KSB Limited, Model MegaCPK 125-100-200

## Lockout / Tagout (LOTO) Procedure for P-7
Before any maintenance on P-7, the following steps are MANDATORY per Factory Act Section 21:
1. Stop the pump from the local control panel LCP-2.
2. Open and lock the main breaker MB-07 in the Motor Control Centre.
3. Apply personal danger tag with technician name and time.
4. Close suction valve SV-7 and discharge valve DV-7.
5. Verify zero energy by attempting a local start (must not run).

## Maintenance History
- 2025-03-12: Mechanical seal replaced. Vibration was 7.1 mm/s before, 2.3 mm/s after.
- 2025-06-04: Bearing DE (drive end) replaced due to abnormal temperature (92°C).
- 2025-09-18: Impeller inspection — minor cavitation pitting noted, within tolerance.

## Known Failure Pattern
P-7 has a recurring drive-end bearing failure roughly every 90 days. Root cause
analysis (RCA-2025-44) attributes this to misalignment after the coupling was
replaced in February 2025. Recommended action: laser alignment at next overhaul.

## Operating Limits
- Max bearing temperature: 85°C (alarm), 95°C (trip)
- Max vibration: 4.5 mm/s (alarm), 7.1 mm/s (trip)
- Rated flow: 320 m³/h at 45 m head
