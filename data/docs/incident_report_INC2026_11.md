# Incident Report — INC-2026-11

Incident ID: INC-2026-11
Date: 2026-04-19
Equipment involved: FD-2 (Forced Draft Fan, Boiler House)
Type: Trip on high bearing temperature — boiler B-3 forced shutdown

## Description
FD-2 tripped at 91°C drive-end bearing temperature during the evening shift.
Because of the IBR interlock, boiler B-3 shut down with it, losing steam supply
for 3.5 hours. Inspection found the DE bearing grease severely degraded.

## Root Cause
Lubrication starvation. The OEM service bulletin FB-2025-09 (received December
2025) revised the required lubrication interval for this fan model from 500
running hours to 250 running hours in high-ash environments. The SOP was never
updated, so crews continued lubricating at 500 hours per SOP-BH-04.

## Corrective Action
1. Lubricate FD-2 bearings every 250 running hours effective immediately.
2. Update SOP-BH-04 to reflect OEM bulletin FB-2025-09. Owner: N. Iyer.
3. Audit all other OEM bulletins received in the last 24 months for similar
   un-applied revisions. Owner: S. Rao.
