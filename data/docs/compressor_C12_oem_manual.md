# OEM Manual Extract — Reciprocating Air Compressor C-12

Equipment Tag: C-12
Manufacturer: Kirloskar Pneumatic, Model KPC-250
Location: Utilities Block, Compressor House

## Operating Limits
- Discharge pressure: 7.5 bar (normal), 9.0 bar (relief valve set point)
- Discharge air temperature: 110°C alarm, 130°C trip
- Lube oil pressure: minimum 1.5 bar (trip below)
- Cooling water flow: minimum 40 L/min

## Recommended Maintenance Intervals
- Air intake filter: clean every 500 running hours, replace every 2000 hours.
- Valve plates: inspect every 4000 hours.
- Lube oil: change every 2000 hours or 6 months, whichever is earlier.

## Safety Notes
- C-12 must be isolated and de-pressurised before any valve work.
- Hot work near C-12 requires a gas clearance certificate due to oil-vapour risk.
