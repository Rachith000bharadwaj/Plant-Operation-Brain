# Safety Inspection Note — Cooling Water Pump House
Inspection Date: 2026-01-15
Inspector: S. Rao (Safety Officer)

## Finding regarding Pump P-7
During routine inspection, it was noted that the current SOP for P-7 lockout does
NOT require closing the discharge valve DV-7 before seal work. However, after the
near-miss incident NM-2026-03 (water hammer during seal replacement), the updated
safety directive now REQUIRES closing BOTH suction and discharge valves.

NOTE: This conflicts with older work permit templates still in circulation, which
only mention the suction valve. All teams must use the updated procedure.
